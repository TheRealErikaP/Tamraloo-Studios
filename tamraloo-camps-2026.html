Common Questions

Where are the camps held?

All camps are at Tamraloo Studios, 2800 Nicolaus Road #100, Lincoln, CA 95648 — next to Empire Gymnastics, easy Highway 65 access. We serve families from Lincoln, Rocklin, Roseville, Granite Bay and all of Placer County.

What's the difference between the All-Day Camp and the weekly camps?

The Creative Conservatory Camp runs all day (9am–3:30pm) every week June 9 through August 1. It includes both the morning performing arts sessions AND the afternoon STEM labs. The themed weekly camps (Pirate, Dance, LEGO, etc.) are either AM only or PM only — and they're also available individually. If you enroll in the all-day camp, those sessions are included.

Do you accept charter school funds?

Yes — Tamraloo Studios is an approved vendor for most Placer County charter schools including South Sutter Charter, Horizon Charter, Clarksville Charter, Feather River Charter, Visions in Education, and more. Call us at (916) 664-2516 to confirm your school and get a purchase order started.

What is the sibling discount?

Families enrolling multiple children receive 10% off the lesser tuition. Sibling discounts apply to weekly and multi-week enrollments and cannot be combined with Early Bird pricing.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations 14+ days before camp: full refund minus a $25 processing fee, or a full credit toward another camp. Cancellations within 14 days: credit only, no refund. No refunds or credits after the first day of camp. Questions? Call (916) 664-2516 or email support@Tamraloo.com.
