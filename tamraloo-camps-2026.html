Common Questions

Where are the camps held?

Tamraloo Studios, 2800 Nicolaus Road #100, Lincoln, CA 95648 — next to Empire Gymnastics, Highway 65 access. We serve families from Lincoln, Rocklin, Roseville, Granite Bay and all of Placer County.

What’s the difference between the All-Day Camp and the weekly camps?

The Creative Conservatory Camp runs all day (9am–3:30pm) every week June 8 through July 31. It includes both morning performing arts AND afternoon STEM sessions. The themed weekly camps are AM or PM only — and if you enroll in all-day, those sessions are included.

Do you accept charter school funds?

Yes — Tamraloo is an approved vendor for Horizon Charter, South Sutter Charter, Clarksville Charter, Feather River Charter, Visions in Education, and more. Call (916) 664-2516 to get a purchase order started.

What is the sibling discount?

10% off the lesser tuition for families enrolling multiple children. Cannot be combined with Early Bird pricing.

Cancellation policy?

14+ days before camp: full refund minus $25 or full credit. Within 14 days: credit only. No refunds after camp begins. Questions? (916) 664-2516 or support@Tamraloo.com
