Creative Conservatory Camp

All-Day  ·  Performing Arts + Creative STEM  ·  Ages 5–12

Tamraloo's signature all-day camp has been completely reimagined.   • Weekly instrument spotlights to help students discover their path before committing to private lessons

  • Flexible early drop-off and extended care available

Ages 12 mo – 5

Music — Early Childhood

Preschool Performers & Storybook Stars

Music and movement programs designed for the youngest learners — building rhythm, listening skills, and a lifelong love of music through play, story, and song. No experience required. No pressure. Just joyful, structured discovery in a warm studio environment.

What to Expect

  • Weekly group music & movement class
  • Rhythm, melody, and song structure through play
  • Age-appropriate instrument exploration
  • Preschool Performers: ages 3–6
  • Storybook Stars: ages 5–8

The Path Forward

Early childhood programs are the first step in a student's musical journey. Students who discover a passion for a specific instrument naturally progress into private lessons in piano, voice, guitar, or any other instrument we offer. Many of Tamraloo's most accomplished older students started right here.

Explore Music Programs
Ages 5 – Adult

Music — Private & Group Instruction

Piano · Voice · Guitar · Drums · Strings · Woodwinds · Ukulele

One-on-one private lessons with degreed, credentialed instructors — each matched carefully to your student's personality, learning style, and goals. Every lesson is a custom experience. Every instructor is a working musician in their field.

What to Expect

  • Weekly 30-minute private lesson (upgradable to 45 or 60 min)
  • Technique, theory, and repertoire — tailored to the student
  • Performance opportunities throughout the year
  • Annual Spring Showcase — memorized solo performance
  • Bel Canto group voice available ages 5–10
  • Instrument rentals available through the studio

The Path Forward

Private lesson students build toward Tamraloo's Spring Showcase — a full solo performance before a live audience. Students preparing for conservatory auditions, scholarship competitions, or pre-professional programs work directly with Tamie Rogers on advanced repertoire and audition strategy.

Explore Music Lessons
Ages 5 – Adult

Dance — Classical & Contemporary

Ballet · Tap · Jazz · Hip-Hop · Musical Theatre Dance · Adult Dance

Technique-first training across every major style — taught by instructors who have performed and trained professionally. Whether your student is stepping into a ballet class for the first time or preparing a dance audition for a performing arts college, our faculty meets them where they are.

What to Expect

  • Weekly group class by style and level
  • Technique, artistry, and injury prevention emphasis
  • Spring Showcase performance opportunities
  • Adult dance classes for all experience levels
  • Musical theatre dance integrates acting and vocals

The Path Forward

Dance students at every level have the opportunity to perform at Tamraloo's seasonal showcases. Advanced students interested in triple-threat (voice, dance, acting) training can progress into Tamraloo's Pre-Professional program — designed specifically for students pursuing scholarship auditions and conservatory programs.

Explore Dance Classes
Ages 6 – 18

Theatre — Musical & Performance

Musical Theatre · Summer Theatre Production · Spring Showcase

Acting, scene work, audition technique, and full musical production experience — taught by instructors with professional stage, film, and conservatory backgrounds. Students learn to perform, not just learn about performing.

What to Expect

  • Musical theatre classes combining acting, singing, and movement
  • Audition technique and cold-reading skills
  • Full Summer Theatre production with live audience
  • Spring Showcase performance — memorized solo or ensemble
  • Stagecraft, confidence, and public speaking built in

The Path Forward

Theatre students progress naturally into Summer Theatre productions and Tamraloo's Pre-Professional Triple Threat program. Students preparing for university auditions, conservatory programs, or competitive performing arts scholarship applications receive targeted guidance from Tamraloo's faculty — including Tamie Rogers, who has coached two consecutive Miss Sac Outstanding Teen winners.

Explore Theatre Programs
Pre-Professional  ·  By Placement

Pre-Professional — Triple Threat Instruction

For Students Pursuing Scholarships, Conservatories & Professional Careers

Tamraloo's most intensive program — designed for serious students who are preparing for conservatory auditions, performing arts scholarships, or professional careers in music, dance, and theatre. Entry is by placement. Instruction is personal, rigorous, and outcome-focused.

What to Expect

  • Voice, dance, and acting training integrated and sequenced
  • Audition repertoire selection and coaching
  • Scholarship application and audition strategy guidance
  • Direct mentorship with Tamie Rogers and senior faculty
  • Performance opportunities in community and regional venues

The Path Forward

This program has produced students who have gone on to win pageant scholarship titles, earn performing arts college acceptances, and build professional performance careers. If your student has a serious goal — a program, a scholarship, a career — this is where we build the plan to get them there. Contact us to schedule a placement consultation.

All School Ages

Academics — Literacy & Mathematics

Literacy Intervention & Enrichment (LIE) · Math Assessment & Intervention

Structured, compassionate academic support grounded in proven intervention methodology. Students have moved from failing grades to thriving. Our credentialed academic instructors work in small settings with personalized learning plans — the same standard of individualized attention we apply to every performing arts lesson.

What to Expect

  • Initial academic assessment to identify gaps and strengths
  • Customized intervention plan per student
  • Weekly sessions with a credentialed instructor
  • Literacy: reading fluency, comprehension, writing
  • Math: 1st grade through college-level, including AP and physics

The Path Forward

Academic programs at Tamraloo are designed to be temporary — the goal is to build genuine, lasting skills so students can thrive independently. Many families who start with academic tutoring discover that their students also benefit from arts instruction, finding that confidence in one area translates powerfully into the other.

Owner & Artistic Director

Artistic Director

Three decades of teaching. A lifelong commitment to her students.

Tamraloo Studios is owned and directed by Tamra Rogers — an Elly Award-winning actress, professional soprano, collaborative pianist, and music educator whose career spans more than 30 years across California and the Pacific Northwest.

Tamie personally trains and mentors every instructor on the Tamraloo team, ensuring consistent quality from a child's very first group class through advanced private coaching toward a conservatory audition.
