Serving Placer County

Music, Dance, Theatre & Tutoring in Lincoln, CA

Tamraloo Studios is Placer County's largest performing arts studio, located at 2800 Nicolaus Road #100 in Lincoln, CA 95648. We offer private and group music lessons, dance classes, theatre programs, academic tutoring, and summer camps for students from toddlers through adults. We serve families throughout Lincoln, Rocklin, Roseville, Granite Bay, Sun City, and the broader Placer County region.

Our music programs include piano lessons, voice lessons, guitar lessons, drum lessons, strings and woodwind lessons, and ukulele lessons — all taught by degreed instructors. Dance classes include ballet, tap, jazz, hip-hop, musical theatre dance, and adult dance classes. Theatre programs include musical theatre training, Summer Theatre productions, and Pre-Professional Triple Threat instruction for students pursuing performing arts scholarships or conservatory programs.
